Our story

Built by a student,
for students

How CareerPrep was born out of rejection, panic, and a lot of Red Bull.

The night before the Goldman interview

It was October 2024. Fredrik Bjørnson, a second-year Master's student at Copenhagen Business School, had just landed a first-round interview at Goldman Sachs Oslo for an M&A Analyst position. It was his dream job. He had three days to prepare.

He Googled "M&A interview questions." 847,000 results. He opened 14 tabs. Nothing was specific to Norway, nothing was tailored to his CV, and nothing told him what Goldman Oslo actually cared about versus what some American blog had copy-pasted from 2011.

"I spent more time looking for the right resources than actually preparing. I bombed the technical round. I couldn't walk through a DCF properly because I'd been reading about the wrong things." — Fredrik

He didn't get the job. But he did get an idea.

The revenge project

Over the following semester, in between lectures on corporate valuation and late-night study sessions at CBS library, Fredrik started building. He interviewed 40+ students who had gone through finance and consulting recruitment in Scandinavia. He spoke to recruiters at DNB, McKinsey Oslo, Deloitte, and PwC. He mapped every step of every process.

The pattern was always the same: students were smart and hardworking, but they were preparing with the wrong tools — generic, non-specific, and not adapted to the Nordic market.

CareerPrep is born

CareerPrep launched in early 2026 as a simple side project. Within three months, 800+ Scandinavian students had used it to prepare for interviews at top firms. One of them landed the Goldman role Fredrik had missed. He sent Fredrik a bottle of Champagne.

Today CareerPrep is the go-to preparation platform for students at BI, NHH, CBS, SSE and other leading Scandinavian business schools — with role-specific prep, live job listings, and an AI mock interviewer that actually reads your CV.

"We're not trying to replace hard work. We're trying to make sure your hard work is pointed in exactly the right direction." — Fredrik Bjørnson, Founder

What's next

We're working on full AI integration (so the mock interviewer actually responds to your specific answers), live job scraping across Finn.no, LinkedIn and Glassdoor, and partnerships with companies so you get insider recruiter data directly on the platform. If you want to be part of it — reach out.
